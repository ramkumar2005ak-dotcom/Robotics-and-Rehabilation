Research & Innovation

Pioneering breakthroughs in humanoid robotics and rehabilitation through cutting-edge research, interdisciplinary collaboration, and technological innovation.

Advanced Humanoid Locomotion

Our research focuses on developing robust bipedal walking algorithms that enable humanoid robots to navigate complex terrains with human-like stability. We've achieved a 40% improvement in balance recovery using predictive control models and dynamic gait optimization.

12+ prototypes · 8 patents pending

AI & Machine Learning for Robotics

We're pushing the boundaries of edge AI with lightweight neural networks that enable real-time perception, decision-making, and adaptive control on embedded systems. Our research has resulted in 47 publications and 5 industry awards.

47 publications · 5 industry awards

Neuro-Rehabilitation & BCI

Our neuro-rehabilitation research combines brain-computer interface (BCI) technology with functional electrical stimulation (FES) to restore motor functions in patients with spinal cord injuries and stroke. Current trials show a 65% improvement in function.

8 active trials · 200+ patients

Clinical Research & Translational Studies

We collaborate with leading medical institutions to translate our research into clinical practice. Our studies include a multi-center RCT on exoskeleton-assisted gait training with 200+ patients across 15 clinical sites.

15 clinical sites · 200+ patients

About NOVA Technologies

We are a deep-tech company with a dual mission: advancing humanoid robotics and transforming rehabilitation. Founded in 2018 by engineers and clinicians, we combine cutting-edge research with clinical expertise to create solutions that improve lives.

Building the Future of Human-Machine Interaction

NOVA Technologies is a pioneering deep-tech company at the intersection of humanoid robotics and rehabilitation medicine. Founded in 2018, we've grown from a small research lab to a global leader in intelligent systems that enhance human capabilities and restore mobility.

Our Mission

To engineer intelligent systems that augment human capabilities, restore mobility for those in need, and push the boundaries of what's possible in robotics and rehabilitation. We are committed to creating technologies that empower individuals, enhance human potential, and contribute to a more inclusive and accessible world.

Our Vision

A world where advanced robotics and rehabilitation technologies are seamlessly integrated into daily life, enabling everyone to live with dignity, independence, and full mobility. We envision a future where human-robot collaboration enhances human capabilities and creates new possibilities for healthcare, industry, and society.

Our Journey

2018

Foundation

NOVA Technologies was founded by a team of engineers and clinicians with a vision to bridge the gap between robotics and rehabilitation medicine.

First Prototype

Developed our first humanoid robot prototype and established research partnerships with leading academic institutions including MIT and Stanford.

Launched clinical trials for our rehabilitation devices in collaboration with Mayo Clinic and Johns Hopkins, marking our entry into healthcare.
